1)RETRACTION OF HEFKER (cont.)

(a)Answer #2: In Beraisa #2, he made it Hefker in front of three people. R. Yosi considers this to be proper Hefker (and he cannot retract). In Beraisa #1, Hefker was done in front of two (or less). This is not Hefker mid'Oraisa (he can retract until someone takes it).

1.(R. Yochanan): Hefker in front of three is Hefker. In front of two it is not Hefker.

(b)(R. Yehoshua ben Levi): Mid'Oraisa, even in front of one person it is Hefker;

1.Chachamim said that it should be done in front of three, in order that one of them could acquire it and the other two could testify to this.

2)PARTNERS WHO VOWED FROM EACH OTHER

(a)(Mishnah): If each of two partners (in a courtyard) vowed not to benefit from the other, neither may enter the courtyard;

(b)R. Eliezer ben Yakov permits them, since each enters his own portion.
