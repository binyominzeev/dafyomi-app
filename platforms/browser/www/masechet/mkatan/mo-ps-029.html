12th CYCLE DEDICATIONS
 
MOED KATAN 29 (20 Nisan) - sponsored by Mr. Martin Fogel of Carlsbad, California, in memory of his father, Yaakov ben Shlomo Fogel, on the day of his Yahrzeit.

1)

THE ENDING

(a)

The Gemara discusses the departing of the soul from the body.

(b)

(R. Levi bar Chaisa): When taking leave of a living person one should say "Go to [your] peace"; when taking leave of a dead person he should say "Go in peace" - but not vice versa.

(c)

(R. Levi): It is a good thing to go right from the synagogue to the Beis Midrash and vice versa.

(d)

(Rav): Torah scholars can expect no rest in the Next World, for they will go from strength to strength.
