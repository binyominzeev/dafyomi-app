1)NEZIRUS ON CONDITION (cont.)

(a)Answer #2 (Abaye): The case is, he said 'even if it is not Ploni, I will be a Nazir';

(b)Question: Why does the Mishnah say 'those whose words were not fulfilled'?

(c)Answer: It means, even one whose first words were not fulfilled, but his latter words were fulfilled (is a Nazir).

(d)(Mishnah): If the approaching man reversed direction, (the first two men) are not Nezirim.

(e)(Gemara): This is only because the man reversed direction. Had we recognized him, (the one who was correct) would be a Nazir!
